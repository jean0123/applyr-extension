How It Works

Three steps to a tailored resume

No signup, no backend, no data collection. Just open a job posting and click.

1

Open a Job Posting

Browse jobs on Indeed or LinkedIn as you normally would. Applyr detects the posting automatically.

2

Click Tailor

Applyr reads the job description, compares it to your resume, and uses AI to create a tailored version.

3

Apply Instantly

Review the tailored resume, download the PDF, or let Applyr auto-upload it directly to your application.
