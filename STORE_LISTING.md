# Applyr - Chrome Web Store Listing

## Short Description (132 characters max)

AI-powered resume tailoring for Indeed & LinkedIn. Auto-customize your resume for every job. Supports Claude, ChatGPT & Gemini.

## Detailed Description

**Stop sending the same generic resume to every job. Applyr tailors your resume to each position in seconds.**

Applyr is a Chrome extension that uses AI to automatically customize your resume for every job you apply to on Indeed and LinkedIn. Instead of spending 20-30 minutes manually tweaking your resume for each application, Applyr analyzes the job description and rewrites your resume to highlight the most relevant skills, experience, and keywords -- giving you a significantly better chance of getting past applicant tracking systems (ATS) and landing interviews.

### How It Works

1. **Browse jobs** on Indeed or LinkedIn as you normally would.
2. **Click the Applyr button** on any job listing.
3. **Applyr reads the job description**, compares it against your base resume, and uses AI to generate a tailored version that emphasizes the qualifications the employer is looking for.
4. **Review the result**, download as PDF, or auto-upload directly to your Indeed application.

### Key Features

**Choose Your AI Provider**
Applyr gives you the freedom to use the AI model you trust most. Connect your own API key for Anthropic Claude, OpenAI ChatGPT, or Google Gemini. Switch between providers at any time.

**Smart Resume Tailoring**
The AI analyzes job requirements, preferred qualifications, and key responsibilities, then restructures and rewrites your resume content to match. It preserves your real experience while optimizing language, emphasis, and keyword alignment.

**Auto-Upload to Indeed**
Save time with one-click resume upload. After Applyr generates your tailored resume, it can automatically attach it to your Indeed application -- no manual downloading and re-uploading required.

**Works on Indeed & LinkedIn**
Applyr integrates directly into the two largest job platforms. The extension detects job listings and provides a seamless in-page experience on both sites.

**Professional PDF Generation**
Every tailored resume is available as a clean, professionally formatted PDF ready for submission.

**Application History & Tracking**
Keep a record of every job you have applied to and every tailored resume you have generated. Review past applications, revisit tailored resumes, and track your job search progress over time.

**Your Data Stays Yours**
Applyr stores all data locally in your browser. Your API keys are encrypted with AES-256-GCM before storage. Resume content and job descriptions are sent only to your chosen AI provider -- never to our servers. There is no analytics, no tracking, and no data collection.

### Pricing

**Completely free.** Bring your own API key and use Applyr at no cost. You only pay for the AI usage through your own provider account. If you enjoy Applyr, you can optionally support the project by buying the developer a coffee.

- Anyone applying to multiple positions and wanting to maximize response rates
- Professionals who want ATS-optimized resumes without hiring a resume writer
